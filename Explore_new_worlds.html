Art / Drawing Project

This was the "Personal" portion of the Golden Cirlce project. I chose reading as the intrest that I wanted to highlight as I thought it would be an interesting concept that could go many ways. This was drawn on Procreate for iPad with an Apple Pencil. To start, I began with a simple book outline then added planets of which I had made stamps for (to make the proscess easier). I then finalized the line art and began colouring. To create the "star dust" waves effect, I drew the waves then coloured over top with a noise brush and finaly cut away extra dust with the selection tool. To create the font, I used another image as inspiration, then invented the letters that the reference didn't have.
