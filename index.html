The Big Picture

What is the Big Picture?

The Big Picture revolves around 3 ideals that I find personally important and that I try to impliment in all my projects: Creativity, Achievement, and Vision. These values are all vital to my work, they help to bring the project together, to give meaning behind it, to make it bigger than itself. Thats the Big Picture. As you go through my previous work and projects, I hope that you will be able to see each of these elements woven throughout each one.
