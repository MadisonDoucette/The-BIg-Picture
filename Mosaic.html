Madison Doucette

Inquiry Hub Mosaic

Art / Drawing Project

The orange, black and white mosaic was created as a way to improve the school and promote the school to future students. It was assigned in the Golden Circle Project as a small, solo poster design project and was taken to the next level with 16 individual posters that are to be displayed as individual posters, or as a 4x4 mosaic which forms the Inquiry Hub logo. The posters follow a simplistic 60-30-10 colour scheme that matches the school colours and switches in each poster for variety and cohesiveness (some posters have white as 30%, others have the orange at 30% etc.). The poster collection has now been adopted into a large wall-mural in the office as well as in the computer lab which has been used for many Live Stream events, including Virtual IHub Open, 2020.
